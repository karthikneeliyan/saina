About us

Saina Call drivers, the brain child of our Managing Directors Mr. M Prosanth Kumar and Mr.P.C Suresh was established on June 17 2018.


M.Prosanth Kumar is a BSC graduate who worked for KLM Royal Dutch Airlines for 27 Years as a Customer Service Officer in Saudi Arabia.


Mr P.C Suresh a B.COM graduate worked in Hindustan Lever as a Marketing Manager for 12 Years, Post which he started his own start up a Business DSA for all multinational Banks in Chennai for 12 Years.

Our Vision

It is to ensure that we satisfy our customer needs on time and give them a safe and pleasurable journey by enabling them with a friend in need.

What we do

Request A Driver

Tell us when and where you need the driver. We will acknowledge your request and assign a driver. We will be keeping you posted on the driver status.

Saina Call Drivers

Once request has been received by us, we will get in touch with you and assign a proficient driver based on your needs on the requested date and time.

Let's Drive

Our driver will be available at your doorstep at the requested date and time to take care of your travel needs. Once your trip is completed he will drop you again and leave.

Our Goals

Our objective is to enable a proper work life balance and a happy environment for our drivers. We understand the ground reality the drivers of today face and we ensure they are happy.
